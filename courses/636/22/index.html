Office hours:

Office hours is the time that instructors set aside outside of class time for students to come and ask questions. I am happy to help you with your homework, project, and other class-related questions or concerns. We will determine office hours together during the first week of class. They will be announced in class and posted on my schedule. If the times we choose don't work for you, send me an email or DM and we'll set up a time that works for you.
