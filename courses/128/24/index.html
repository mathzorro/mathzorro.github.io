Office Hours:

Office hours is the time that instructors set aside outside of class time for students to come and ask questions. The exact hours will be determined by group consensus during the first full week of class and announced in class, as well as posted on my schedule. If those times don't work for you, send me an email or DM and we'll set up a time that works for you.
