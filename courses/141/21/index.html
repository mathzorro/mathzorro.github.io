Office hours:

Office hours is the time that instructors set aside outside of class time for students to come and ask questions. I am happy to help you with your homework or other class-related questions or concerns. This semester, I will have online office hours, which we will determine the first week of class. They will be announced in class and posted on my schedule. If those times don't work for you, send me an email or DM and we'll set up a time that works for you.
